### Course Overview

This lab accompanies the core Cognitive Psychology lecture course (PSYC 4011). Students engage in hands-on experiments and data analysis related to topics such as attention, memory, language, perception, and decision-making. The lab emphasizes experimental design, scientific writing, and critical thinking.

### My Role

As the lab instructor, I will guide students through weekly sessions focused on replicating classic studies, analyzing cognitive data, and writing APA-style lab reports. I also support students in developing their own small-scale experiments.

### Skills Emphasized

- Experimental methods in cognitive psychology  
- Data interpretation and analysis  
- Research communication and scientific writing  
- Replication and open science practices
